Privacy Policy

Data Collection and Storage

BL-POS is designed with your privacy in mind. Our app operates entirely offline and does not collect or transmit any personal data to external servers. All information, including:

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information. The app does not require an internet connection to function, ensuring your data remains private and secure.
